About Me

I'm Adel Rahimi, student of English Language Teaching, interested in Computational Linguistics. My research interests include: Intonational Phonology, Computational Phonology, Experimental Phonology, Speech Recognition, Speech Perception, Computational Linguistics, Natural Language Processing, and Corpus Linguistics.     Dakhil wordlist for Persian vocabulary

    The Dakhil Wordlist consists of roughly 260K words from Persian (though there may be some duplicates) ending in "ان" "ات" "ون" "ین" but it is not part of their morphological boundary. the wordlist has been uploaded in both the affixes in one txt file and separated by their ending.
    F or more information read the paper: Rahimi, Adel. "A new hybrid stemming algorithm for Persian." arXiv preprint arXiv:1507.03077 (2015).

  • txt format

    • Rahimi, Adel , 2015, "Dakhil wordlist for Persian vocabulary", DOI:10.7910/DVN/MJBHLN, Harvard Dataverse, V1

    Corpus of Computational Linguistics' Academic Literature

    The corpus of Computational linguistics is an 8 million corpus of Journal publications, books, and theses. these include interdisciplinary topics such as Speech Recognition, Experimental Phonology, Language Models, Machine Learning, Semantics, Syntactic Theory, and Information Retrieval.

  • txt format
  • 8 million words

    • Rahimi, Adel , 2015, "Corpus of Computational Linguistics' Academic Literature", DOI:10.7910/DVN/YHHTCI, Harvard Dataverse, V2
